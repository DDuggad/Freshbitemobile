Take an existing web app called "FreshBite" and redesign it into a highly interactive, mobile-first UI in Figma.

CONTEXT:
FreshBite is a Bangalore-based surplus food marketplace connecting PURE VEG restaurants with users. Customers reserve discounted food and pick it up. Vendors manage deals via dashboard.

IMPORTANT:
- NO delivery features
- Focus on "Reserve", "Pickup", "Save Food"
- Region: Bangalore, India (₹ currency, Indian UI patterns)

----------------------------------
